No more juggling multiple INI files like ddraw.ini or f2_res.ini. All settings are accessible directly inside the game through an integrated preferences interface or via ‘fallout2.cfg’.

Existing scripts, mods, and art assets built for vanilla Fallout 2 (and CE) still work. The vanilla game, or TCs that worked with the original .exe, work with FISSION.

True widescreen support, scalable interfaces, and consistent behavior across platforms — macOS, Windows, Linux, and more — without undoing the original experience.

Every design choice respects the pacing, tone, and intent of the original Fallout titles. The goal is preservation — not reinvention.

A cleaner, easier setup means players and fans can configure, understand, and modify the engine without digging through undocumented hacks.
